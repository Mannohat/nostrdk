## We need your voice

Nostr is an exciting protocol, but there is very little content about it in Danish. If you have experience with Nostr, Bitcoin, decentralized social media, or you're simply curious and would like to share your thoughts, we would love for you to contribute a post here on Nostr.dk.

## What can you write about?

It can be anything from:

- Your first experiences with Nostr
- A guide to getting started
- A review of a particular client or service
- Thoughts on censorship resistance and freedom of speech
- Bitcoin and Lightning combined with Nostr

## How to contribute

Send us an email at [nostr@nostr.dk](mailto:nostr@nostr.dk) with your post or an idea for a post, and we'll figure it out together.

We look forward to hearing from you! 💜
